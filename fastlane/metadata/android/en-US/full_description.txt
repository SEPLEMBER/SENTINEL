Minimalistic app that records time lapse videos in the background with the screen turned off.
Time lapse videos can be saved in various resolutions as a MP4 video file, or as individual JPEG files.
While recording there is no preview visible and the screen can be turned off.
There is no recording limit, but the app will stop recording if battery level or disk space is low.

Features:

* Record normal video (MP4)
* Record time lapse video (MP4/JPEG)
* Select various resolutions
* Select camera to use
* Schedule recording on specific date
* Preview with selected resolution
* Disable shutter sound
